About this study

Thank you for your interest!

This is a study by the University of Oxford on choices people make. We will present you with a situation and ask you to make choices.

Your choices and the choices of the other participants will determine how much you earn. We will explain how this works during the session.

 

There are a couple of things that are good to know:

  • The session lasts 10-15 minutes.
  • Your earnings are kept private and are paid through Prolific Academic.
  • You will earn at least 0.85 pounds for your participation, provided you complete the study and participate actively. The average earnings are around 2.00 pounds.
  • We will not reveal who you are to other participants.
  • We never deceive participants. For example, if we inform you that another participant is making a choice on which you can react, this is indeed the case. We keep our promises made to participants. For example, if we promise a certain payment, participants will indeed receive it.
  • The data collected in this session will be used for the research study and might be published, both online or offline. No data that allows people to identify you will be published without your permission.
  • Participation is voluntary and you can withdraw at any time. If anything makes you feel uncomfortable, let us know. Our study has been reviewed and approved by the Ethics Committee in Oxford. You can find the details at the bottom of this page.

In case you want to get in touch with us, you can message us through the internal messaging system of Prolific Academic.
